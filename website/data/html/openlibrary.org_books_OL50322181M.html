Verlos ons van het kwaad
Edit

Thriller with A Shaw as the hero. The storyline is good, action packed and keeps you turning the pages. This is a story where a British organization researchers find, track and take down evil individuals( in this story An Ukraine devil). Frank's organization team's up with them. A nice exciting story.
